Step-by-Step Campaign Setup

  1. Create Campaign — Go to Ads Manager → Create → Choose objective based on funnel stage:
    • Ads 1 & 2 (Cold): Use Awareness or Traffic objective
    • Ads 3 & 4 (Mid/Consideration): Use Engagement or Traffic with landing page view optimization
    • Ads 5 & 6 (Bottom/Education): Use Leads or Conversions with "Complete Registration" event
  2. Set Budget — Start with $50-100/day per ad set. Use CBO (Campaign Budget Optimization) if running 3+ ad sets.
  3. Create Ad Set — Select audience (see targeting below). Set placements to Advantage+ or manual Feed + Stories. Schedule for 7-day learning phase minimum.
  4. Create Ad — Upload the 1:1 creative image. Paste the Primary Text, Headline, and Link Description from each ad card above. Set CTA button. Enter the Deep Link URL.
  5. Tracking — Install Meta Pixel on mynucleus.com. Set up Conversions API (CAPI) for server-side events. Verify "Lead" and "CompleteRegistration" events fire on form submission.
  6. Review & Publish — Preview in all placements. Check mobile rendering. Submit for review (typically approved within 24 hours for health-adjacent content).

Audience Targeting by Funnel Stage

🔵 Cold — Awareness (Ads 1 & 2)

Interest targeting: IVF, In Vitro Fertilization, Fertility Treatments, Reproductive Medicine, Genetic Testing, Prenatal Care

Demographics: Women 28-42 • Household income top 25% • Engaged or Married

Lookalikes: 1-3% LAL based on website visitors or lead list

Exclusions: Existing customers, past purchasers

🟡 Consideration (Ads 3 & 4)

Retargeting: Website visitors (last 30 days) who viewed /ivf, /embryo, or /preview pages

Engagement: People who engaged with your Instagram/Facebook content in last 60 days

Video viewers: 50%+ video viewers from awareness campaigns

Exclusions: People who already submitted a lead form

🔴 Bottom-Funnel (Ad 5) & 🟣 Education (Ad 6)

High-intent retargeting: Website visitors who viewed pricing page or clicked "Get Started"

Cart abandoners: People who started but didn't complete the consultation booking

Broad for education: Ad 6 can also run broad to biohacker/longevity interest audiences (e.g., Bryan Johnson, Peter Attia, longevity, epigenetics)

Exclusions: Converted leads, existing patients
